What is Digital Law?

Digital law refers to the rules, rights, and legal responsibilities that apply to the use of technology, the internet, and digital information. It is one part of digital citizenship focused specifically on what is legal or illegal online. -Chat GPT

What is AUP?

An AUP is a set of rules that explains how people are allowed to use a computer system, network, internet service, device, or online platform. Schools, companies, and organizations use AUPs to promote safe, legal, and responsible technology use. - Chat GPT

What is Plagerism?

Plagiarism is presenting someone else's work, ideas, words, images, or creations as your own without giving proper credit. -Chat GPT

What is academic dishonesty?

Academic dishonesty is any action that gives a student an unfair academic advantage or misrepresents their learning or work. -Chat GPT
